## Brand Identity

### Your Unique Angle (Why People Will Follow You)

Everyone is talking about AI coding. Almost nobody has **20 months of production evidence** across **40+ services** to back it up. Your differentiator:

| What Everyone Else Says | What You Say (With Evidence) |
|------------------------|------------------------------|
| "AI will replace developers" | "AI is a force multiplier — but only with structure" |
| "Just vibe code it" | "Session 30 proved that vibe coding fails in enterprise. Here's the data." |
| "Use AI to move fast" | "Moving fast without methodology cost us a full week. Revert-Prove-Rebuild saved us." |
| "Here are 10 AI tips" | "Here are 72 sessions of production data showing what actually works" |
| Theory and speculation | Battle-tested framework with case studies |

### Voice & Tone

- **Authoritative but approachable** — you're the practitioner, not the pundit
- **Data-first** — lead with numbers, follow with narrative
- **Contrarian where earned** — challenge the hype, but always with evidence
- **Generous** — give away the methodology freely (the repo IS the product)
- **Storytelling** — every data point has a story behind it

### Key Phrases to Repeat (Brand Anchors)

Use these consistently to build recognition:
- "This is not vibe coding."
- "Trust but verify — AI output is a draft, not gospel."
- "72 sessions. 2,938 interactions. 40+ production services. Here's what I learned."
- "The AI didn't fail. The methodology did."
- "Deep Onboarding before you touch a single line."
- "5 review rounds or it doesn't ship."
- "DLQ everything." (will resonate with backend engineers)
- "The 10 Commandments of AI-Assisted Development"

## Engagement Playbook

### Daily Habits (15 min/day)

1. **Respond to every comment** on your posts within 2 hours
2. **Comment on 3-5 related posts** by other AI/engineering leaders (add genuine value, not "Great post!")
3. **Bookmark/save interesting AI failures** shared by others — you'll reference these later
4. **Track metrics weekly**: impressions, engagement rate, follower growth, repo stars

### Engagement Triggers

When someone posts about:
- "AI replaced my job" → Share your evidence that it's augmentation, not replacement
- "I built X with AI in 1 hour" → Ask "How many review rounds?" (genuinely, not sarcastically)
- "AI coding is overhyped" → Agree partially, then share how methodology makes it work
- "Vibe coding" (positively) → Share Session 30's failure as a friendly counter-narrative
- "AI generated bad code" → "That's not the AI's fault — it's a methodology gap. Here's what works."

### People to Engage With (Build Your Network)

Target accounts in these categories:
- **Engineering leaders** sharing AI adoption stories (VP Eng, CTOs, Staff Engineers)
- **AI developer tools** founders (they'll amplify your methodology content)
- **Tech conference speakers** on AI topics (future speaking opportunity pipeline)
- **Other practitioners** sharing real-world AI coding experiences (not consultants/influencers)
